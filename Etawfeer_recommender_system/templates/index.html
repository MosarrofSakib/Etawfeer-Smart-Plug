Welcome to E-Tawfeer Recommender System

E-Tawfeer is an intelligent energy-saving recommender system designed to monitor your device usage and help you reduce electricity consumption efficiently. Whether you’re at home or away, E-Tawfeer keeps track of how your appliances behave — and recommends smart actions like turning them off when idle or when you're not present.

The system uses real-time power readings from connected smart plugs or energy monitors, applies machine learning models to detect patterns, and classifies behaviors into energy-saving categories (Micro-Moment classes). Based on the detected class, it generates personalized notifications with economic and environmental insights to guide better usage.

Recommendation messages are sent directly to the user's mobile phone through the Home Assistant mobile app. From there, users can accept or reject the recommendation with a single tap. Their response is logged in the system and used to improve future recommendations.

The system also visualizes your power consumption trends over the last 24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days to help you stay aware of your habits and track your impact.

💡 Pro tip: You can create automations, view personalized energy recommendations, and take actions — all from this dashboard.
